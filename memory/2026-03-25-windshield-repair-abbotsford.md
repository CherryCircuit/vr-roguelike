**The process — here's the good news, it's pretty painless:**

1. **Report the claim to ICBC first** — call **1-800-910-4222** or do it online at icbc.com/claim or through the MyICBC app. You'll need your policy number. Glass claims are approved almost immediately.

2. **Then take it to the shop** — ICBC-approved shops bill ICBC directly, so you don't pay out of pocket. No deductible for glass claims on basic coverage, and it won't affect your premiums.

3. **Call ahead and book** — since it's a large crack (likely a full replacement rather than a repair), the shop will need to order the windshield. A replacement takes 1-2 days typically, so you'd probably leave the vehicle. Call first to confirm they have the glass in stock or can get it quickly.

4. **Don't drive it if the crack obstructs your view** — that's an MVA violation and a fine. If it's in your line of sight, consider mobile repair options or get it towed/flat-bedded.

**TL;DR order of operations:**
1. Call ICBC (1-800-910-4222) → get claim number
2. Call the glass shop → book appointment, ask if they have the windshield in stock
3. Drop the car off → they bill ICBC directly → you pay nothing
