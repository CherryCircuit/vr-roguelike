## What MIYOSMART Is

They're specialized lenses using **D.I.M.S. technology** (Defocus Incorporated Multiple Segments) — developed with Hong Kong Polytechnic University. They correct vision like normal glasses *and* have a ring-shaped treatment zone that slows myopia progression simultaneously. They look and fit like regular glasses, no special frames needed.

## The Science

This isn't marketing fluff — there's serious clinical backing:
- **2-year RCT**: slowed myopia progression by **~60%** vs. standard glasses
- **6-year follow-up**: effect sustained, no rebound after stopping
- **8-year follow-up**: average total progression under **-0.50D** over the entire 8 years (which is remarkable)
- **Newer MiYOSMART iQ**: even better — 6-month trial showed **no progression on average**, and is ~2x more effective than the original
- Over **90 peer-reviewed publications** — the most of any myopia management spectacle lens

## The "Free Upgrade" Warranty — What's Actually True

This is where it gets nuanced. The warranty is **not** a blanket "free lenses forever if prescription goes up":

- **Prescription change warranty**: 1-year, **one-time free replacement** if prescription changes by ≥0.50D
- **Coating defect warranty**: 2 years, one-time replacement
- **Satisfaction guarantee**: 120 days

**The Eye-M-Growing program** (Canada-specific) offers **two pairs for the price of one** (e.g., clear + sunglasses, or a backup pair), with the second pair available immediately or within 16 months. But it's positioned as a "multi-pair package," not unlimited free replacements.

So the "they cover upgrades" claim is **partially true** — you get one free prescription-change replacement in year one, and potentially a second pair through EMG. After that, you're paying again.
